American Greetings

Generally Americans are apt to use the verbal, so Americans always say "Hi" or "How are you?" Even people who are strangers say "How is it going?"



Japanese Greetings

Japanese people tend to use nonverbal language. Many Japanese just make a bow with a smile indicating respect. Usually, young people have to make a deep bow for their superiors. In addition, not only gesture but also voice and countenance are very significant.
